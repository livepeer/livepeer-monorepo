### How to delete a stream

<p><font color="red">Deleting a stream cannot be undone.</font></p>

When a stream is deleted, the **stream name** is not reusable and the **stream session** history is erased.

To delete a single stream:

1. Navigate to the **stream page** for the stream you want to delete. 
1. Click the button to **delete** the stream and follow the prompt to confirm.

To delete multiple streams:

1. Navigate to the **streams list page**
1. Click the radio button next to the streams you want to delete
1. Click the **delete** button and follow the prompt to confirm.
